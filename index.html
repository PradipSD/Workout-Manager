Weight progression rule

Double progression: stay at a weight until every set hits the top of the rep range, then add 1–2.5kg (upper body) or 2.5–5kg (lower body) and drop back to the bottom of the range.

Reps badhao jab tak asaan na lage, phir weight badhao.

When you fail a set

Missed reps → rest 90s extra, retry. Form breaks down → cut weight 10% for remaining sets. Repeat the same weight next session until it's clean.

Kabhi bhi form todke reps mat nikalo.

Nutrition target

~3000 kcal/day, ~120g protein (~2.3g/kg bodyweight). Surplus is the other half of this program — training alone won't move the scale.

Surplus ke bina best program bhi kaam nahi karega.
